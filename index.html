About Us

We are Tech Con 2023, the premier technology convention of the year! Join us at the Las Vegas Convention Center from June 12th to June 15th, 2023, for an unforgettable experience. Tech Con 2023 is the perfect opportunity for tech enthusiasts to learn about the latest trends and innovations in the tech industry. Our three-day event will feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, and workshops, all designed to help you stay ahead of the curve. You’ll have the opportunity to network with other tech enthusiasts and industry professionals, and explore the latest products and services in our exhibition hall. The Las Vegas Convention Center is a state-of-the-art facility that is well-equipped to host large-scale events like Tech Con 2023. Located in the heart of Las Vegas, it is easily accessible by public transportation. Don’t miss out on this exciting event that will bring together the best and brightest minds in the tech industry. Register now and join us for an unforgettable experience!
