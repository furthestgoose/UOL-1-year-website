Tim Cook is an American business executive who is the CEO of Apple Inc since 2011. He succeeded Steve Jobs as the CEO of Apple and has led the company’s growth, innovation, and social impact. Cook is also known for his advocacy of environmental and social causes.

Mark Zuckerburg

Founder and CEO of Meta (Formerly known as Facebook)

Harvard University

More

Mark Zuckerberg is an American business magnate, computer programmer, internet entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is the co-founder, executive chairman, and CEO of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook, Inc.) . Zuckerberg attended Harvard University, where he launched Facebook in February 2004 with his roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes . He has since used his funds to organize multiple philanthropic endeavors, including the establishment of the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative .

Jeff Bezos

CEO of Amazon and CEO of BlueOrigin

Princeton University

More

Jeff Bezos is an American entrepreneur, media proprietor and investor. He is the founder, executive chairman, and former president and CEO of Amazon, the world’s largest e-commerce and cloud computing company. He is also the founder of Blue Origin, a spaceflight company, and the co-founder of Bezos Expeditions, a venture capital firm. Bezos graduated from Princeton University in 1986 with degrees in electrical engineering and computer science.

Bill Gates

Harvard University

More

Bill Gates is an American entrepreneur, software developer, and philanthropist. He is the co-founder of Microsoft, the world’s largest personal-computer software company. Gates has also been involved in various philanthropic activities through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, which he co-founded with his wife Melinda Gates. Gates has been awarded numerous honors for his philanthropic work and contributions to the field of technology.
